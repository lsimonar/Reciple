How to play

Guess the daily RECIPLE in 6 tries.

Each guess must be one of the world's most popular dishes. Click the enter button to submit an attempt.

After each guess, the tiles will turn to show the five main ingredients of the guessed dish - colored green if they coincide with these of the solution.
